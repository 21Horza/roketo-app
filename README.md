Roketo app
==================

This app is built to immitate the real use case for Roketo streaming service (https://www.roke.to/).
Demo is available at https://21horza.github.io/roketo-app/


How to use
===========

1. Register your testnet NEAR account at https://wallet.testnet.near.org/ and (or) log in
<img width="710" alt="Screenshot 2022-07-30 at 18 54 24" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/81642088/181908320-f041c4fd-53e2-4c74-88a6-13c1d81b1d6e.png">

2. Click DEPOSIT button and add some amount of NEAR tokens (1 NEAR is enough)
<img width="1440" alt="Screenshot 2022-07-30 at 19 14 00" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/81642088/181908477-d3f81a3b-9a1d-47ed-ae32-ebc9bc9be60d.png">

3. Now you can click START and fill in all the information (amount, time in mins and seconds)
<img width="1440" alt="Screenshot 2022-07-30 at 19 15 02" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/81642088/181908506-3909b512-d2fa-4a77-96d6-d8c38ed83bc8.png">

4. Go ahead and click start buttons to immitate the training
![GifGym (2)](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/81642088/181908858-85e16f54-5c57-45bc-98c0-a1477bd6c281.gif)

5. If time is up, you need to restart the process by adding some NEAR tokens and setting timer again
